Our Community Mission

At Mount Pleasant Baptist Church, we believe in serving our community beyond our sanctuary walls. We are committed to making a positive impact in Barry and the surrounding areas through various outreach programs and charitable initiatives.

Our mission is to show God's love through action, supporting those in need and working toward a more compassionate and connected community.
