Our Worship Experience

At Mount Pleasant Baptist Church in Barry, we believe that worship through music is a powerful expression of our faith and devotion to God. Music has always been at the heart of our community, bringing us together in unity and lifting our spirits in praise.

We gather as a community of believers to worship God through vibrant music, heartfelt teaching, and genuine fellowship. Whether through traditional hymns or contemporary worship songs, every note is offered as an act of worship to glorify His name.

Our Vision Our vision is to know Jesus better and to make Him better known, so that Cadoxton and the world might be transformed through His love. Music and worship are central to this mission, creating an atmosphere where hearts are opened and lives are changed.

Get Involved

If you have a passion for music and worship, we'd love to have you join our worship team. From vocalists to instrumentalists, there are many ways to serve and use your gifts to glorify God and bless our congregation.

For more information about our music ministry or to get involved, please speak with any member of our pastoral team during or after Sunday service. We look forward to worshipping with you!
