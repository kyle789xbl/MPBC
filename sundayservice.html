About Our Service

Baptisms

Baptism is a special moment of commitment and celebration in our faith community. If you're interested in being baptized or would like to learn more about what baptism means within our church, we'd love to talk with you.

Baptisms are an important part of our worship life and we celebrate this meaningful step with our whole community. If you'd like to participate or have questions, please speak with any of our pastoral team members during or after Sunday service.
