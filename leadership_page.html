Our Mission & Vision

At Mount Pleasant Baptist Church, our mission is to know Jesus better and to make Him better known, so that Cadoxton and the world might be transformed through His love.

Our Vision We are committed to creating a welcoming community where people can grow in faith, serve one another, and reach out to our local area with the hope and love of Jesus Christ.
