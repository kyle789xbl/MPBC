Our Seasonal Events

Mount Pleasant Baptist Church celebrates the seasons with special events that bring our community together. Each event offers a unique opportunity to worship, connect, and serve one another.

Harvest Family Service

Our Harvest Family Service is a celebration of thanksgiving and community. This beloved event includes:

  • A service in the Community Hall
  • Gathering food for the Vale Food Bank to support local families in need
  • Family-friendly crafts for all ages
  • Children's song performances
Photo Gallery: Abbey has photos from this year's service and previous years available for viewing.

Christingle Service

A meaningful service celebrating light and hope in our community.

Carols by Candlelight

Join us for an atmospheric evening of festive carols and candlelit worship.

Christmas Day Service

A short, special service on Christmas morning at 10:30 am to celebrate the birth of Christ together as a church family.

Tabernacle Feast

A wonderful new addition to our seasonal calendar, the Tabernacle Feast was held for the first time this year on 4th October 2025, marking our return to this tradition after COVID. We hope this will become a regular annual event.
